You clicked a link from an email that appeared to require review of a company document or agreement. This page is part of MyRepsoft's internal Security Awareness Training activity.

Important Security Reminder

Before clicking links in emails, always check the sender, the message context, the URL, and whether the request is expected. Attackers often use urgent or official-looking messages to trick users into clicking links.

What you should do next time

  1. Check if the sender address is expected and legitimate.
  2. Do not click links from suspicious or unexpected emails.
  3. Do not enter passwords, MFA codes, or company information on unknown pages.
  4. Report suspicious emails immediately to security@myrepsoft.com.
